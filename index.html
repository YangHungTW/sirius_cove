About Us

“Financial literacy is a combination of awareness, knowledge, skill, attitude and behaviour necessary to make sound financial decisions and ultimately achieve individual financial wellbeing" the OECD.

Sirius Cove Partners operates an ecosystem that breeds ideas, leads the financial education journey, guiding an investor through a decision process. The ecosystem uses AI to optimise user experience with behavioural nudges.

We are all unique, it’s what makes us all special, there is no other you. What’s important to you may not be important to someone else. Your financial situation means something to you. Creating the financial future you want begins with understanding yourself and the biases that drive your decisions.

Sirius Cove Partners is a privately owned business that collaborates with its sister companies Square 1 Research and Research IP to produce unique retail consumer products, to empower decision making.

Services

Sirius Cove Partners works closely with its Taiwan sister company Square 1 Research. Square 1 Research provides products to the Taiwan governments Funds Rich Service (FRS). FRS establishes a direct to consumer research based financial services platform, that aims to improve the retirement savings of Taiwan retail investors. Square 1 Research products and services are used by over 38,000 retail investors.

Sirius Cove Partners also leverage the services of FundSource, wholly owned by the New Zealand Stock Exchange (NZX) qualitative research provider, Research IP. Research IP’s qualitative research is viewed by over 4,500 New Zealand financial advisers and retail consumer each month.
